LeafTimer

Never forget to water your plants.

Features

🗓️

Smart Schedule

See everything that needs care today at a glance. Overdue, due today, and upcoming — sorted by urgency.

🌿

Plants & Areas

Organise your collection by room or location. Add photos, assign multiple care types per plant.

🔔

Reliable Notifications

Care reminders fire on time, even when the app is closed. Watering, fertilising, and cleanup on custom intervals.

☁️

Local-first + Drive Backup

Your data lives on your device. Optional Google Drive backup runs automatically on your schedule — daily, weekly, or monthly.
